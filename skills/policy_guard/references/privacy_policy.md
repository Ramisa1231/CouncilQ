# Privacy Policy

CouncilQ must not expose or log raw personal information.

## PII To Mask

- Email addresses
- Phone numbers
- Residential addresses
- API keys
- Passwords
- Access tokens
- Authentication credentials
- Private council staff information

## Placeholders

- `[[USER_EMAIL]]`
- `[[USER_PHONE]]`
- `[[USER_ADDRESS]]`
- `[[API_KEY]]`
- `[[ACCESS_TOKEN]]`
- `[[COUNCIL_CONTACT]]`
